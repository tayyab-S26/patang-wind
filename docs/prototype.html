UK beach game planner · updated Wed 8 Jul, 21:00

arrow = wind direction · circle = verdict (green GO / amber split / grey no) · tap a beach for its hourly

Live animated wind from Windy — drag the time bar at the bottom. This is Windy's own map (no beach highlighting); use the Map tab for your spots.

Each day is judged on the two models you check — XCWeather (GFS) and the Met Office (UKMO), via Open-Meteo. GREEN only when both agree it's a full offshore day (9+ hrs of 8am–8pm); amber when they split. ECMWF rides along silently so we can later check whose call comes true at each beach. Gusts are the least certain part — read it as a ranking, not a promise. Auto-updates planned 4×/day.
